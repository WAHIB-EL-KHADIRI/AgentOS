# Code of Conduct

AgentOS aims to be a focused, respectful engineering community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Discuss ideas directly without attacking people.
- Give clear technical feedback.
- Assume good intent when possible.
- Welcome beginners who are willing to learn.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Threats or intimidation
- Spam or repeated off-topic promotion
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or
block participants who harm the community.
